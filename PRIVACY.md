# Privacy Policy

The canonical, always-current privacy policy for **Web Clipper for OmniFocus**
lives at:

**https://clip.spiralocean.com/privacy**

In short: the extension collects nothing, makes no network requests, and sends
your clips only to OmniFocus on your own Mac. Follow the link above for the full
policy, including a per-permission breakdown.
