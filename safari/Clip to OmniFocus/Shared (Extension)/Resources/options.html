Clip to OmniFocus

Defaults

Clips go to your OmniFocus Inbox when the project field is left blank. Project and tag names must match your database exactly.

Requirements

Clip to OmniFocus requires macOS and OmniFocus for Mac installed. It is an unofficial extension and is not affiliated with or endorsed by The Omni Group.

OmniFocus and the OmniFocus icon are trademarks of The Omni Group.

If clipping fails, confirm OmniFocus is installed and that Chrome is allowed to open external applications when prompted.

Quick clip shortcuts

  • ⌘⇧O — clip current page
  • ⌘⇧S — clip selected text

You can also right-click a page or text selection and choose Clip to OmniFocus.

Chrome may ask once to let Clip to OmniFocus open OmniFocus. That approval covers every site — you do not need to allow individual websites.
