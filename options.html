Web Clipper for OmniFocus

Defaults

Clips go to your OmniFocus Inbox when the project field is left blank. Project and tag names must match your database exactly.

Requirements

Web Clipper for OmniFocus requires macOS and OmniFocus for Mac installed. It is an unofficial extension and is not affiliated with or endorsed by The Omni Group.

OmniFocus and the OmniFocus icon are trademarks of The Omni Group.

If clipping fails, confirm OmniFocus is installed and that Chrome is allowed to open external applications when prompted.

Keyboard shortcuts

    Chrome requires each shortcut to include ⌘ or Ctrl — for example ⌘⇧O or ⌘⌥O. A combination without one of those (such as ⌃⌥ alone) will not record.

    You can also right-click a page and choose Clip page to OmniFocus, or right-click a text selection and choose Clip selection to OmniFocus.

    Chrome may ask once to let Web Clipper for OmniFocus open OmniFocus. That approval covers every site — you do not need to allow individual websites.
